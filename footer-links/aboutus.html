About Us



Hello and welcome to DBN Books Public Library. We are a public library established in the year 2022 serving the community of Ottawa but open to all Ontarian's and Canadian's alike. We strive to provide the best reading experience and want to give our members the best environment for their reading needs.

Since recently established, we currently have a plethora of reading materials to choose from, but with that being said we strongly encourage anyone who may have a particular book in mind that we currently do not have in our inventory to request whichever title is in their interest through our "Contact Us" section and our BDN ADMIN team will work quickly to bring the requested literature to our shelves and online platform.
